Do it Yourself Dog Wash:

Washing your pet in your bathtub isn't something most people look forward to. BarkWash provides raised tubs, so you can avoid all that bending over and hurting your back. We provide shampoos and conditioners, towels, brushes, ear and eye wipes, and blow dryers. Our tubs can accommodate dogs of all sizes!


Our DIY stations are first come, first serve, with a 90 minute time limit! Take a look next time you visit us for your grooming needs!
